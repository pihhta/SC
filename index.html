ABOUT PRODUCT

SIBERIANCUSTOMS made AKM pattern wood set Izhmash Red finish

Nearly identical clone of the original Soviet AKM wood furniture. All of the features are present, look and feel, down to the smallest details and even has that "surplus smell".

Main features:

-- Same quality as the Soviet Laminate material is usedv

-- Same wood texture, cut lines and visual appearance as the original

-- All metal parts used are NOS from the good old days

-- 100% functioning cleaning kit compartment

-- 3 piece lower hand guard

-- "bent" laminate upper hand guard, so there will be no cracking even when installed on the tightest gas tube

-- The color and the dept and the quality of the finish is indistinguishable from  the original Soviet surplus (proprietary Soviet formula was preserved in the family of one of the partners of SIBERIANCUSTOMS)
